Project Next Steps: Stakeholder Feedback

Review the proposed next steps below and share your input. All comments are synced live from Google Sheets.

Option 1: Delay the Rotary involvement in Window Rock for one school year, then reevaluate next year

This option would be funded by NightMoves and would work with the school to get another group of students engaged in the program. It would also demonstrate Window Rocks commitment to the MOU of which many steps were missed last year (e.g., Computer support, allocated class time, Sophomores or Juniors in the class).

Option 2: End the Rotary participation in this project

Unfortunately this program did not have the success this year as we hoped. Most of the students were seniors and only 1 senior and 2 juniors will continue into the next school year. The support from Window Rock did not meet the MOU and it is not clear if they will next year. Use the effort as a lessons learned and know that many students did benefit from the NightMoves and Brainwise training but not to the extent as we proposed in the Global Grant.

Stakeholder Input

Option 3: Redirect the effort to another school.

This would potentially let us leverage the lessons learned at Window Rock and get momentum at a different school. Linc has identified a school that has a strong STEM and workforce focus that is Native American run (Window Rock is an AZ public school). There is a risk that we only have 1 year of funding.
